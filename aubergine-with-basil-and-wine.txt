2 - 3 onions (2 very large or 3 medium)
1 large western eggplant or equivalent of thin eggplants.
Olive Oil
1 tsp thyme
1 tsb oregano
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 1/2 tbs Basil
3 tablespoons soy sauce
2 cups red wine - preferably dregs with yeast


Cut onion into halves, then cut thin slices.
Cut eggplant into 1x3x1/2 in slices

Cook onion oil in a generous amount in an open pan in olive oil until 
transparent.

Add thyme, oregano, basil and garlic powder, cover and let cook for 45 seconds
to a minute.

Add eggplant.

Add 3 tablespoons of Soy Sauce.

Cover with red wine and cook until most of the liquid is evaporated and/or absorbed.

Add additional herbs as needed.

Serve over brown rice.
